# North Star — launch-week

**Goal:** Ship the public beta and land the first 10 signups.
**Heartbeats:** 10

## What "done" looks like
- The beta is live behind a feature flag, signup works end to end.
- A launch post is published with a working demo link.
- 10 researched prospects have been contacted (drafts approved by the CEO).

## Out of scope
- Billing, teams/seats, the polished dashboard. Beta first, polish later.
